Barroilhet Avenue. Where do I start?

I had a friend who lived in this spacious, empty house that they had to themselves more often than any teenager should. Each house is basically closed off and theres all types of houses. This house had a pink transluscent sculpture of a woman's body in the backyard. Only the hallways lights ever stayed on, these songs playing through their fathers sound system in his office. We would just pass through each room like ghosts. Barroilhet Ave isn't just a street to me; it's a experimental limbo of over honesty, moping, and early unwanted independence.
